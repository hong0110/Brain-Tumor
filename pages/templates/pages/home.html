This AI model uses ResNet-50 to detect brain tumors from MRI images with high accuracy and fast processing speed.
ResNet-50 is a powerful deep neural network for image classification, especially effective with medical images like brain MRIs. It can detect brain tumors and differentiate between benign and malignant types. With high accuracy (90%-97%), it helps identify abnormalities that are hard to detect visually, offering promising support for early and accurate diagnosis using AI.
